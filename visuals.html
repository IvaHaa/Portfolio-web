About my visual work

I greatly enjoy creating any visual materials, from posters for events to complicated abstract paintings.

I work mainly with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator (for vector work I also use Inkscape ), but have dabbled with many different tools online and offline. I'm mostly self-taught, but have had courses in vector work and basic painting skills. I've also learned how to work with 3D art in 3DsMax.
